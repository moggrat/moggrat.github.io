New Blog, First Post & Updates

Sat, 03 Apr 2021

Hello reader. I'm Luke a web/game developer from NW England, and this is my blog. The purpose of this blog is for me to talk about the ideas behind my games/projects/updates. Project being tools, websites and just kinda anything. I also want to talk a bit about myself and my thoughts between and/or as part of other updates. I'm still unsure how often this blog will be updated.

Onto what I have been up to:

All my maintained sites are undergoing minor refactoring. This site has had some aesthetic changes too; in addition to the implementation of this blog.

The other site that is going to receive a large update is my "Civilization VI Shuffle & Randomization" site. The new design should work much better on mobile devices. The current site (for some reason) is missing a viewport meta tag. The "Regional Civilization Randomizer" has been updated for the new "New Frontier" pack (Portugal Civilization and Zombie game mode). However, the Map randomizer is out of date. I plan to retire the map randomizer; I may leave this as a legacy page on the site in the next update. I'll be making another blog post when I finish this update.

Regarding game development, I haven't been too active on my game. I will focus on game development once I've finished all my website updates. In the past, I perhaps would have said "I'll wait till I have more time", but I need to make time. On another note, later in April, I may join a game jam, so I'm looking forward to that.

Thanks for reading; I hope future posts will be more insightful.
